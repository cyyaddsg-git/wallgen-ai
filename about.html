WallGen AI turns your imagination into desktop and phone wallpapers. No sign-ups, no watermarks, no paywalls — just you, a prompt, and a beautiful result.

The story

It started with a simple frustration — every time I searched for a wallpaper that matched exactly what I had in mind, I ended up with the same overused stock photos, or sites that demanded a login, a subscription, and my firstborn child.

So I built WallGen AI. One page, one input, one click. Type what you dream up, pick a resolution, and seconds later it's yours. Forever.

Always free, and here's why

Running WallGen AI costs real money — servers, bandwidth, and the AI models behind the scenes. I could've put up a paywall. I could've demanded sign-ups and sold your data. I didn't, and I never will.

Instead, I rely on something simple and honest: ads. Small, unobtrusive banners that pay a fraction of a cent each time someone like you visits. That's it. That's the whole business model.
